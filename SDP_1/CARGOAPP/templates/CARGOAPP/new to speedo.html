New To SPEDOO

SPEDOO Corp (SPEDOO) is a provider of transportation, e-commerce and business services. It offers time-definite delivery services and international trade services such as customs brokerage, and global ocean and air freight forwarding. The company provides small-package ground delivery services, residential delivery services, less-than-truckload (LTL) freight services, in-store services and other business solutions. SPEDOO also offers technical support, information technology, communications, and billing and collection services. The company operates under the brand names SPEDOO, SPEDOO Express, SPEDOO Ground, SPEDOO Freight, SPEDOO Office, SPEDOO Logistics, SPEDOO Office, SPEDOO Dataworks and SPEDOO Services. It has a market presence across various countries and territories.
